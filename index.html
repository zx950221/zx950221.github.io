Essential Apps Guide - Exploring China 2025

China is a paradise for mobile payments and apps! Having these essential apps ready before departure will make your trip more convenient and smooth. Here's the latest 2025 China travel essential app guide:

WeChat (微信)

All-in-One Social & Payment App

WeChat is China's most popular social media and payment platform, covering nearly every aspect of daily life - from communication and payments to food ordering, ride-hailing, and even hotel bookings.

Setup Steps:

  1. Download & Install:
    • iOS App Store
    • Google Play Store
  2. Register Account:
    • Use your phone number to register (we recommend using your regular phone number)
    • Complete verification code validation
    • Set a password and complete basic profile information
  3. Activate WeChat Pay International (Tourist Wallet):
    • Open WeChat, tap on "Me" in the bottom corner
    • Tap "Services," then find "Wallet"
    • Select "WeChat Pay for Overseas Users"
    • Follow the prompts to link your international credit card (supports Visa, MasterCard, etc.)
    • Complete identity verification
    • Set up a payment password

Usage Tips:

  • In China, scanning merchant QR codes for payment is very common
  • Use the "Scan" feature to quickly add friends or view information
  • Utilize WeChat's built-in translation feature to help with communication
  • Add your hotel, guide, or travel agency's WeChat contact information in advance

Alipay (支付宝)

Leading Mobile Payment Platform

Alipay is another major payment platform in China, accepted by almost all merchants. It also offers a "Tour Pass" service specifically designed for tourists.

Setup Steps:

  1. Download & Install:
    • iOS App Store
    • Google Play Store
  2. Register Account:
    • Register using your phone number or email
    • Complete verification code validation
    • Set a login password
  3. Set up Tour Pass (Tourist Wallet):
    • Open Alipay and switch to English interface
    • Click on "Tour Pass" or "Prepaid Card"
    • Enter the amount you want to add (recommended: 1000-2000 CNY)
    • Link your international credit card to complete the top-up
    • Set up a payment password

Usage Tips:

  • Alipay Tour Pass is valid for 90 days, so please add funds according to your trip duration
  • Show your "Payment Code" for merchants to scan, or scan the merchant's QR code to complete payment
  • Alipay's "Translation" feature can help you overcome language barriers during your trip
  • The app also includes useful features like subway transit codes and ride-hailing

Amap (高德地图)

China's Best Navigation App

In China, Google Maps services are unstable. Amap is the best navigation alternative, offering precise real-time route planning, public transit navigation, and ride-hailing services.

Setup Steps:

  1. Download & Install:
    • iOS App Store
    • Google Play Store
  2. Basic Setup:
    • Allow location permissions when first opening the app
    • You can use guest mode directly without registering an account
    • Switch to English interface in settings (Settings > Language > English)

Usage Tips:

  • Supports offline map downloads - we recommend downloading maps of cities you plan to visit in advance
  • You can directly search for attractions, restaurants, hotels, etc. on the map
  • Provides accurate public transportation routes, including subway and bus options
  • Integrates ride-hailing functionality, allowing you to call cars directly within the app

DiDi (滴滴出行)

China's Uber

DiDi is China's largest ride-hailing platform, covering most cities nationwide and providing international tourists with convenient transportation options.

Setup Steps:

  1. Download & Install:
    • iOS App Store
    • Google Play Store
  2. Register Account:
    • Register using your phone number
    • Complete verification code validation
    • Complete basic information
  3. Set Up Payment Method:
    • You can link WeChat Pay or Alipay
    • Or directly link an international credit card (limited support, we recommend using the first two methods)

Usage Tips:

  • DiDi international version supports English interface, making it easy for foreign travelers to use
  • Save frequently used addresses in advance, such as hotels and major attractions
  • Use the app's built-in translation feature to communicate with drivers
  • If you already have WeChat, you can also use DiDi services directly through the WeChat mini-program

Dianping (大众点评)

China's Yelp+Groupon

Dianping is the best app for finding restaurants, purchasing group-buying vouchers, and discovering local hotspots, providing you with authentic user reviews and discount information.

Setup Steps:

  1. Download & Install:
    • iOS App Store
    • Android App Markets
  2. Register Account:
    • You can log in directly using your WeChat or Alipay account
    • Or register a new account with your phone number

Usage Tips:

  • Use the filter function to find restaurants that match your taste and budget
  • Check "Popular Rankings" to discover local popular restaurants and attractions
  • Purchase "Group-Buying Vouchers" to enjoy discounted prices
  • Use a mobile translation app to help understand Chinese reviews

Xiaohongshu (小红书)

Discover Trending Places & Travel Inspiration

Xiaohongshu is a social platform where Chinese youth share life experiences, and it's also an excellent resource for finding Instagram-worthy spots, unique cafes, and off-the-beaten-path attractions.

Setup Steps:

  1. Download & Install:
    • iOS App Store
    • Google Play Store
  2. Register Account:
    • You can register using your phone number, WeChat, or Apple ID
    • Complete basic information and interest selection

Usage Tips:

  • Enter city name + keywords like attractions/coffee/food in the search box
  • Check recently published posts for the latest information
  • Use the map feature to see popular spots nearby
  • Save your favorite posts to collections to plan your itinerary
